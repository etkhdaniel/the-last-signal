Privacy & Advertising

Return to The Last Signal

Local game saves

The game stores progress in your browser using local storage. Save data remains on your device unless you copy and share an exported save.

Advertising

When advertising is enabled, third-party advertising providers such as Google may use cookies, local storage, device information, or similar technologies to serve, measure, and personalize advertisements. Advertising providers operate under their own privacy policies.

Current configuration

The advertisement loader remains inactive until valid publisher and ad-unit identifiers are added to js/ad-config.js. Before that configuration is completed, the site does not load the Google AdSense script.

Game analytics

The game does not currently include a separate analytics service. Repository hosting and advertising providers may collect standard technical information according to their own terms.

Questions

Questions or concerns can be submitted through the project’s GitHub issue tracker.
