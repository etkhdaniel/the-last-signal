# Roadmap

The first playable chapter establishes the core engine and story arc. Future releases can expand the game without changing the architecture.

## Chapter 2 — The Inland Network

- 15 additional locations
- Faction reputation and branching quests
- Equipment slots and item rarity
- Crafting recipes and field consumables
- Five new bosses

## Chapter 3 — The Silent Coast

- 15 additional locations
- Boat and weather systems
- Settlement construction
- Companion characters
- Three additional endings

## Endgame — Beyond the Carrier

- 10+ late-game locations
- Prestige / New Game+ transmissions
- Procedural expeditions
- Challenge modes and achievements
- Community-contributed encounters and ASCII scenes

## Long-term content targets

- 50+ locations
- 150+ upgrades and craftable items
- 100+ ASCII scenes
- 30+ enemies
- 20+ bosses
- Multiple factions and endings
- 20–50 hours of progression
